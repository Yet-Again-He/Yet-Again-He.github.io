Adûnaic Noun Inflections

 

Below are the four categories of nouns in Adûnaic.

Strong-I, Strong-II, Weak-I, Weak-II are each further divided by Neuter and gender inflections.

 

Strong Noun I: ending in a consonant preceded by a short vowel

Dual

Dual-Subjective

khiblât

narkât

 

[1].  So long as it belongs to the Strong-I category, it does not matter what kind of ending vowel and consonant is used, it will always go through the exact same inflections.
