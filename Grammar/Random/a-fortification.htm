a-fortification.

A process by which the variable vowel in a Strong noun is strengthened by changing it to an equivalent long vowel. [1]

Used mostly for the Subjective inflection and to indicate proper noun formation of common nouns.

 

Variable noun changes

a → â,

i → ê,

u → ô.

 

[1].  If you wish for an in-depth explanation for such a phenomenon, look up Paul Strack’s Eldamo, a scholarly collection of every word ever made by Tolkien, also includes grammar.
