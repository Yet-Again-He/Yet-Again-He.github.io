Do bear in mind that a significant part of this is conjecture and will most certainly be subject to change as common consensus is reached.

All forms that were inscribed by Tolkien are marked with an [*].

– pharzunîn, pharaz + nin- (CVCVC + CVVC)

– gimilnitîr, gimil + nitir- (CVCVC + CVCVVC)
