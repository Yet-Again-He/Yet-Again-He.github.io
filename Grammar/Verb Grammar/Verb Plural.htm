Verb Plural

As in many languages (except English), Adûnaic verbs reflect the number of the modified noun.  Indicating its number though is quite simple.

Verb Plural

Add -m to the end of the verb.

 

Examples:

-Nardûwîm yunâkhim, “the soldiers, they are coming”.

-rabîm lugnam, “the dogs swallow.”

-balîka yanuttam! “the ships, they have sunk!”
