23. Grammar

Negation.

*bâ particle. “no, not, don’t”

*-ada prep. “to, towards, against”

*-ak prep. “away”

-bê prep. “as, like”

*-dalad prep. “under, beneath”

*-mâ prep. “with”

*-nad prep. “hind, behind”

*-nud prep. “on”

*-ô prep. “from, so”

*-ôb prep. “fore, before”

-sil prep. “in”

-us prep. “for”

*-zê prep. “at”

*(h)a- pron. “it, its”

*(h)u- pron. “he, him, his”
