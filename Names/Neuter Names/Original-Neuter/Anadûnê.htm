Anadûnê.  “Westernesse”

The Adûnaic name for Númenor, a feminization of anadûni (“western”), though Christopher Tolkien states it to be a loan word from Sindarin Andúnë, a statement indirectly true as they are both are either related, or is from the same Sindarin root word dûn (“west”).
