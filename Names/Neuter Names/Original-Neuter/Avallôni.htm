Avallôni.  “Haven of the Gods”

Adûnaic name for Tol Eressëa, the lonely isle right before the land of Aman, where most of the elven exiles settled, following the defeat of Morgoth. It is hypothetized that the double represents a undescribed rule of compounding, where if the last two sounds of the first element and the first two elements of the second element in a compound are the same, they are superimposed over each other. this results in the vowels absorbing each other while the consonants are left doubled.
