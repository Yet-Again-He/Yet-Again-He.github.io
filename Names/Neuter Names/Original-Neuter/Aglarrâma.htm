*Aglarrâma.  “Castle of the Sea, (lit.) Castle of glory”

No one is sure why Tolkien glossed it “Castle of the Sea” considering he already had a different word for sea, but it could be a hidden reference to Ar-Pharazôn’s state of mind at the end of the Akallabêth.
