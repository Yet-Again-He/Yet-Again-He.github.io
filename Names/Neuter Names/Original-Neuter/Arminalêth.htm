Arminalêth.  “Royal Heaven (City)”

The Adûnaic name for the capital city of Númenor, which is better known by its Quenya name Armenelos (“City of the Kings, (lit.) Noble Heaven City”).  

 

 

Elements

 

Ar-

“ruler (king or queen)”

minal

“sky, the heavens”

-êth

“feminine suffix”
