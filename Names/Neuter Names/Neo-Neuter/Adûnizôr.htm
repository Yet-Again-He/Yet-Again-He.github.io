Adûnizôr.  “Flame of the West”

Adûnaic translation for Andúril, the new sword of Aragorn in the Lord of the Rings.

It was originally forged by the famous dwarf Telchar of Nogrod and named narsil, the “Red and White Flame.”  It was broken at the finale of the War of the Last Alliance, and remained broken for over three thousand years before being reforged on the orders of Elrond Half-elven, and given its new name Andúril, “Flame of the West”.
