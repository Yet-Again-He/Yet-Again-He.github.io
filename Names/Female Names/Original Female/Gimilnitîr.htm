*Gimilnitîr.  “Star-kindler”

A title for Varda the Vala, since she was the one to make the stars to welcome the children of Ilúvatar (men as well as elves).

 

First element is gimil (“all stars”) and second is an agental-formation of the verb nitir (“to kindle”).

 

 

Elements

 

gimil

“(all) stars”

nitir

“to kindle”
