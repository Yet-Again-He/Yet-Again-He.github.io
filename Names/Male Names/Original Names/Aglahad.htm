Aglahad.  “Unglossed”

Tolkien wrote that the Lords of Dol Amroth used Adûnaic for their names, but never provided any translation for much of them.  For all we know, the composition of the above name is either agla + had, or aglaha + d (the second one being the combination of a name plus masculine consonant).
