Belzagar.  “Bright Sword”

Adûnaic name of the 18th ruler of Númenor (his full title being Ar-Bêlzagar), whose Quenya title was Tar-calmacil (“Light Sword”).  His name does use the classical [ê], which in combination with his time, it could perhaps signify that the Númenóreans were in mid-transition from Primitive to Classical Adûnaic.
