Zigûr.  “The Wizard”

Adûnaic name/title for Sauron when he was in his fair form deceiving Ar-Pharazôn and almost all of Númenor.

The very fact they called him that possibly indicated that with the exception of the faithful, the Númenóreans either didn’t know or didn’t care who Sauron used to be, which would have only lent wings to his treacherous plans.

 

 

Elements
