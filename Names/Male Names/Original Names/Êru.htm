Êru.  “God, the Omnipotent”

Adûnaic name for the God of Tolkien’s universe, Eru in Quenya.

What is interesting to note is that this name was first coined for Adûnaic, only later being modified by Tolkien for addition to Quenya, a reverse of his original usual trend.
