√ZUL root. “rise, ascend”

Reverse-derived from *azûl (“east”). Hypothetically from extending the root √ʔUL (“Reflection”) with the Root-extension Z-. This would have resulted in √ZUʔUL, which would have contracted due to the loss of the middle consonant into its present form.
