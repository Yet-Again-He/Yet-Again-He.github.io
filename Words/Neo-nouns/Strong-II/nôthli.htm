nôthli n.  (Strong-II Common) “family, kindred, folk, clan, (fig.) house”

Derived from the Edain word nothlir (“folk, family”), which in turn is a cognate of Sindarin nos(s) (“family, kindred, clan, house; race, tribe, people”).
