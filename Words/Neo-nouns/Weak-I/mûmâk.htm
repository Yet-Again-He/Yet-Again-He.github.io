mûmâk n.  (Weak-I Gendered-Masculine) “male Oliphaunt/elephant”

From the neo-root √MUM (“astonishment, astounded”), formed using the gendered -k/-th agental formation on the adjectival root.

For female Oliphaunt/elephant, see mûmâth.

For oliphaunt/elephant, see Weak-II/mûmâ.htm">mûmâ.
