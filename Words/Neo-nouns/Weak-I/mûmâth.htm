mûmâth n.  (Weak-I Gendered-Feminine) “female oliphaunt/elephant”

From the root √MUM (“astonishment, astounded”), formed using the gendered -k/-th agental formation on the adjectival root.

For male oliphaunt/elephant, see mûmâk.

For oliphaunt/elephant, see Weak-II/mûmâ.htm">mûmâ.
