*Nimir n.  (Strong-I Gendered-Common) “Elf, (lit.) Shining One”

Interestingly, the plural could be taken to mean either “Elves”, “Shining Ones,” or “the Beautiful.”

 

For female elf, see Nimrî.

For male elf, see Nimrû.
