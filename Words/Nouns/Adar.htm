adar n.  “traveler, pilgrim”

Reversed from Adrahil, and bearing great similarities to gnomish adr(a), which means "Situated on the far side."  Similar to Adûnaic -ada, which means "to, towards."

 

Variations

·        adra (such as in Adrahil.  Only used for prefixes for the purpose of fluidity)
