*asdi adj.  (Weak-I Neuter) “full”

To be more precise, the original word was coined by Tolkien, but was never assigned a meaning, nor could one be reverse-engineered.  I gave it the current meaning because it seemed to flow very well in the phrase asdi-y-an listê (“full of grace”).
