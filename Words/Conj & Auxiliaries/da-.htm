da- pref. “agental suffix”

Unlike its suffix equivalent -ân, this can be applied to verbs AND nouns, so long as either are triconsonantal-based.

Biconsonantal and others are not allowed to use this.

This is also the only time in Adûnaic when a word can have more than three consonants (the exceptions being constructed words like kathuphazgân).

 

How it works (WIP)

It could be a comparative/superlative indicator.

 

Noun Examples

Gimil à da-gmil,

 

Verb Examples

Kalab à da-klab,
