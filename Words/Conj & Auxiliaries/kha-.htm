kha- pref. “interrogative particle”

a particle used as a prefix with the appropriate postposition in order to express an interrogative.

 

Interrogatives:

Pronouns

khân, “who?”

khâ(wa), “what?”

Pro-adverb

khâzê, “where?”

khâmâ, “how, with what?”

khâd(a), “whither?”

khâu/khâyô, “from where, whence?”

khânud, “whereon?”

khâdalad, “whereunder?”

khâyôb, “before what?”

khânad, “behind what?”

xxxx, “when?”

xxxx, “why?”

Determiner

akkhân, “whose?”

akkhâwa, “which?”

akkhazutha, “how many, (lit.) of the reckon?”

xxxxx, “what kind of/of what kind?”
