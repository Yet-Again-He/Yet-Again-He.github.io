This is a collection of articles united inside one lexicon in a quest to provide a complete source for all things Adûnaic, an invented language by J.R.R Tolkien, author of Lord of the Rings.  Within, you will not only find every scrap of knowledge written by Tolkien himself, but reams of study derived from his works by multiple scholars to render it useable in more locations beyond the pages of a book.

This lexicon is based and traces its source to the Eldamo Lexicon put together by Paul Strack under the Creative Commons (Attributions) License, which allows you to use this content in whatever way you wish, provide that you credit the author.
