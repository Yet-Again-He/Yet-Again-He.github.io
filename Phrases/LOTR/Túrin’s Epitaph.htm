Túrin’s Epitaph

The inscription on his tomb, translated/transliterated into Adûnaic.

 

Original:

Túrin Turambar Dagnir Glaurunga

Túrin Turambar, Bane of Glaurung

Translated:

Tûrinun Angârû, agan ‘n Galôrungô

Túrin the King of Fate, death of Glaurung

 

Original Name: Glaurung

Transliterations: Galôrung-û/ô (leaning towards second option with second option)
