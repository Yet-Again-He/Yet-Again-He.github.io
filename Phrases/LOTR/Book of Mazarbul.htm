Mazarbul Translation

The last page of the book of Mazarbul, read aloud by Gandalf in Lord of the Rings.

 

Translation

Bâ nêyadamak.  Bâ nêyadamak.

We cannot get out.  We cannot get out.

Yaphazgam abâur kaSâtta Kuzum. 

They have taken the bridge and the Second Hall.

Fârû, Lôni, Nâli kallabam îzê (bravely) nêyadambê mazarbûlada.

Frár and Lóni and Náli fell there bravely while the rest retreated to Mazarbul.

Nêkanam îdô miyikuzum kamaha kitha îdô.

We still hold the chamber, but hope is fading now.

Tôr anÔin yaddam (five days), ka(today) iri tharan môrram.

Óin’s party went five days ago but today only four returned.

Zarâma tabda parag Adûnsôth-zê.

The pool is up to the wall at the West-gate.

Tudânan phazgam Ôin -- Bâ nêyadamak.

The Watcher in the Water took Óin -- We cannot get out. 

Nêga nakha.

The end comes soon.

Nêhuznudam dûmî, dûmî akhâs-sil.

We hear drums, drums in the deep.

…Yanâkhim.

…They are coming.
