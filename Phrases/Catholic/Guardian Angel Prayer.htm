Guardian Angel Prayer

A Catholic prayer for interceding with our individual guardian angel.

 

Avalôn 'nÊru, hê haldân izrê

Angel of God, my Guardian dear,

Hânada zir anÊru kana hê îzê,

to whom God's love commits me here,

katha halla, bata hêyal,

Ever this day, be at my side,

Bila ka(guide), phaza kathurmada, iznêd.

To light and guide, to rule and guard, amen.

adahalla, hêruna kada Êruwad!

Today I begin to live for God!
