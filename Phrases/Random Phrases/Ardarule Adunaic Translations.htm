Yukirribam

“Nê Thôrim ‘n Anadûnê!

“We are the sons of Númenor!

Anadûnê tâidô aglar!

Númenor the Glorious!

Anadûnê tâidô abar…

Númenor the once enduring…

Anadûnê Akallabêth, Hi-kallaba.

Númenor Akallabêth, ‘She that has fallen.’

daba izgil abârram wê-zagîr

Let remorse strengthen your blades.”

Rô ninayam Dôlgû rahtâda katha?

“Will we allow the Darkness to destroy all?

Îdô zatala azgara Mulkhêrada!

Now is the hour to wage war against Morgoth!”

Once More to the Breach

Tê Ancalimë, phêl anErendis.  Kiman?

“I am Ancalimë, daughter of Erendis.  And you are?”

 

Wikanam zêr anagan tê zîrânîyada, kadô wê anga udûn!

“You hold a wish of death against my beloved ones, and so your doom is hell!”

 

Du-gimlîya bilam kê makhal-nud, Ar-Minalbênû.  Ênni ‘ntê Îbal.

“May the stars shine upon your authority, Tar-Meneldur.  My name is Îbal.”

 

Bâ rô anâ dulza agan ‘ntê, Bawbuthôr thôr ‘nUlbar.

“No man will mourn your death, Bawbuthôr son of Ulbar.”
