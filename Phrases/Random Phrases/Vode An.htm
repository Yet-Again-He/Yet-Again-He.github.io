Vode An

A Mandalorian battle hymn, translated to Adûnaic.

 

Vode An

Kote!

Aglar!

Îr abâr Vidrê, Uddôi katha.

One indomitable heart, Brothers all.

Nê, Rakha ‘nCôrusân, Uddôi katha.

We, the Wrath of Coruscant, Brothers all.

Kâ aglâr, mîdôrô aglar,

And glory, eternal glory,

Nê rô kanam burud sullu.

We shall bear its weight together.

Zagâr tagga udûn-sil, Uddôi katha.

Forged like the saber in the fires of death, brothers all.

Yâ nêyôb nitram gimil zôrîmâ.

Those who stand before us light the night sky in flame.

Rûy 'nnê nitram bêlat.

Our vengeance burns brighter still.

Katha yazgâim rô kalbam.

Every last traitorous soul shall kneel.
