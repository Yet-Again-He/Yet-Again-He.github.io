Chevalier, Mult Estes Guariz

A twelth-century marching song originally composed in old french.

Shown here in Adûnaic, English, and its original Old French.

 

Chevalier, Mult Estes Guariz

Nardûn, tunga zûri îzê,

Chevalier, Mult Estes Guariz,

“Knight, salvation is here,”

Î Êrun kîyad maga hû rûkh,

Quant Deu a vus fait sa clamur,

“When God to you made his clamor,”

TurkîAmôrawîyât

Des Turs e des Amoraviz,

“The Turks and the Almoravids,”

Yadibdam hû mûlî aglarî.

Ki li unt fait tels deshenors.

“They greatly dishonored him.”

Mulôk-mâ yaphazgam zâin 'hhû;

Cher a tort unt ses fieuz saisiz;

“Unjustly they have seized His fiefs;”

Atharâ zâukh burôda nênud,

Bien en devums aveir dolur,

“It's just that we suffer,”

zêyad-bê Êru êra binna

Cher la fud Deu primes servi,

“For it was there that God was first served,”

kasapphada ârû-bê

E reconuu per Segnurr.

“And recognized as lord.”

 

îdô Khân yada Lûvid-mâ

Ki ore irat od Loovis,

“He who nows goes with Louis,”

Rô bâ kana ugur 'nUdûn,

ja mar d’Enfern n’avarat pouur,

“Will not fear hell again,”

Manôn rô yada minalada,

char s’alme en iert en Pareïs,

“For his soul will go to Paradise,”

avalôim-mâ khôr annê.

od les angles nostre Segnor.

“With the angels of our lord.”
